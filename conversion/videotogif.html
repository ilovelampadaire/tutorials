How to convert video to gif

1. Pick your video (Can be any video formats as long as convertio.co Supports it and make sure the video is below 60 seconds (Recommended)).

2. Go to convertio.co (It's should look like this):

3. Click "Upload a file" button (Red button) and choose the video file.

4. After choosing the file, upload it to convertio.co (It should look like this):

5. Click the white button (The 3 dots button) and search for "gif" (It's should look like this):

6. Click the "gif" button and click the big red button ("Convert" button) and wait for the file to convert.

Final step 7. Finally, click the "download" button (Blue button) and you're done :D

(Warning: the final gif file could be low quality but this cannot be fixed sadly)
